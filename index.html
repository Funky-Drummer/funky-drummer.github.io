Chiptunes

These ones are made in various programs, with the same general premise. If you don’t know what chiptunes are, basically, it is a term for songs played on sound chips from old gaming devices. Old as in old old. Back when the Famicom, or the NES was around, the GameBoy, Atari, stuff like that, those are chiptunes. And yes, the music from the games are also chiptunes, obviously.

Unnamed Chiptune 1

This was made using FamiStudio. Yes, I know BeepBox is a perfectly fine way to make chiptunes, and I'm honestly better there than here, but I got FamiStudio so I could practice programming an NES sound chip or some similar type of sound chip. Plus, I just think chiptunes are just really creative.
